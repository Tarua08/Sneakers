Sneakers Intro

Beginner-friendly guide to buying your first quality sneakers.

Start Learning

1) Define Your Use Case

  • Daily wear: comfort + durability
  • Gym/training: stability + grip
  • Lifestyle/fashion: silhouette + colorway

2) Budget Smartly

Use this rule:

  • ₹4k–₹8k: great starter range
  • ₹8k–₹15k: premium comfort/style
  • Avoid hype buys before fit/comfort confidence

3) Fit & Sizing Basics

  • Measure both feet at evening time
  • Keep thumb-width toe space
  • Check brand-specific size chart (not only your usual size)

4) Authenticity Checklist

  • Buy from official site / trusted marketplaces
  • Check box label consistency (SKU, size, model code)
  • Inspect stitching, glue finish, logo placement
  • Compare to official product photos

5) Care Tips

  • Use shoe trees
  • Clean gently after wear
  • Air-dry only; avoid direct heat
